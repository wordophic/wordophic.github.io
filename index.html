5) To cope up with eye-sight problems, the 20-20-20 rule is going to be your savior. Stare/focus on an object every 20 minutes at 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
6) Perform eye yoga frequently. Sit straight with your shoulders off. And rotate your eyes in a clockwise and anticlockwise direction. Remember not to abandon focus. Focus on the image along your direction and continue within the loop.
7) Blink your eyes slowly every time as it keeps moist and permits eligibility to focus longer.
8) Try to write on the walls rotating your lens along the direction of alphabets.
