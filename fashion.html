  Posted by: Eman Shaikh   June 12,2020
What summers suggest you to wear?

Light colors always add charm to this hot weather. The pleasure they provide you almost fulfills your beauty looks for the particular season. Let’s have a glimpse of how cool they look when worn with the flow of trend. So, the neon and poppy colors are always elegant to pick for your wardrobe. Furthermore, when short lengthened outfits match with the capris, they end up providing the extreme glow to anyone. Adding to the fashion-sense, wearing the studs always proves to be the masterpiece of your look. Another inn-trend of this season is wearing lime colored tops with wide-legs (pants). Adding the lightest piece of your necklace would however enhance the worth. For bringing the glow of weather to glow of soul, one can also apt picking the T-shirts with boyfriend fit (treggings) to enjoy the summer while staying trendy.

About us

Welcome to Wordophic! It is the brainchild of Adnan Khan and Eman Ahmad Shaikh which highlights different flavors of life. As far as its journey is concerned, Wordophic commenced when we identified the lack of space for writers and prosers in publishing industry. "I once tried to publish a magazine but didn 't know the publication process." -Eman Ahmad Shaikh. This idea popped up the next significant step of Wordophic in mind. It took some days in research to grow Pakistan's online ecosystem. Wordophic has dogged belief that the right to be published should not be fourwalled or limited upto certain writing topics but to be secular in writings. So the contents are written by our in-house team which showcases its work and beget healthy discussions. Wordophic also helps students master public writing signature, building self-esteem and learn about literature and narration. .
