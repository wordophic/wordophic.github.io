What summers suggest you to wear

The three-year old Netflix documentary by Kip Andersen, the filmmaker reveals the secret to prevent and even reverse the chronic diseases. Moreover, he critiques the health impact of ... meat and investigates the serious health problems of the world due to the causes of meat-intake and dairy products. Due to the harmful effects of food pollution, WHO considers the processed meat and tobacco a carcinogen (but this does not mean they are equally dangerous). Therefore, cutting them out can improve our health. If each of us goes a plant-based diet, none of us would be ill. There will also be a profound effect on society. Furthermore, ‘cow milk’ is good for us; still there is a lack of evidence on its relation with strong bones. The film exposes the corrupt government agencies that back the scientific studies, along with big businesses that obviously earn more than trillion healthcare dollars while keeping us sick. The aim of this documentary revolves around exposing such governmental organizations that propagate false narratives to make money. However, there is a dire need to investigate the long-term effects of vegan diet and scrutinize the studies on veganism that are also backed by vegan-based corporations.

About us

Welcome to Wordophic! It is the brainchild of Adnan Khan and Eman Ahmad Shaikh which highlights different flavors of life. As far as its journey is concerned, Wordophic commenced when we identified the lack of space for writers and prosers in publishing industry. "I once tried to publish a magazine but didn 't know the publication process." -Eman Ahmad Shaikh. This idea popped up the next significant step of Wordophic in mind. It took some days in research to grow Pakistan's online ecosystem. Wordophic has dogged belief that the right to be published should not be fourwalled or limited upto certain writing topics but to be secular in writings. So the contents are written by our in-house team which showcases its work and beget healthy discussions. Wordophic also helps students master public writing signature, building self-esteem and learn about literature and narration. .
