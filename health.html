How to improve your eyesight and focus?

People are gradually being aware of the eyes-exercises and have begun calling it a natural remedy to cure eyesight problems. If you are facing any problem of near-sightedness or far-sightedness, ... cataracts, or macular-degeneration due to aging, then you are probably going to benefit from the following exercises:
1) Focus on your point finger a few inches away from your eyes and gradually increase the distance. Remember to keep focusing, and bring that finger slowly towards your eyes. Do it 3 times at least to improve your focus.
2) Create a pinhole with your finger and focus the images through it. This exercise will help you to strengthen your concentration.
3) Focus on your thumb ten-inches away from your eyes for 15 seconds. Then instantly find an object 10-15 feet away and focus on it. Repeat it five times.
4) Trace an imaginary figure 8 with your eyes on the floor. Let the distance be 10 feet. Practice it 30 times, constantly.
5) To cope up with eye-sight problems, the 20-20-20 rule is going to be your savior. Stare/focus on an object every 20 minutes at 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
6) Perform eye yoga frequently. Sit straight with your shoulders off. And rotate your eyes in a clockwise and anticlockwise direction. Remember not to abandon focus. Focus on the image along your direction and continue within the loop.
7) Blink your eyes slowly every time as it keeps moist and permits eligibility to focus longer.
8) Try to write on the walls rotating your lens along the direction of alphabets.
9) Palming your eyes is another efficient tip to relax your eyes. Put your light palms of both hands on your eyes and press them cordially with a light thrust.
